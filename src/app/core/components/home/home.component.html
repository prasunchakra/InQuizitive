National Youth Day

Test your knowledge about the life, teachings, and philosophy of Swami Vivekananda on the occasion of National Youth Day.

National Youth Day in India is observed on January 12 every year to honor the birth anniversary of Swami Vivekananda. He was a key figure in the introduction of Indian philosophies of Vedanta and Yoga to the Western world.

By taking this quiz, you'll learn more about his teachings, his thoughts on youth empowerment, and his contributions towards social reform.
